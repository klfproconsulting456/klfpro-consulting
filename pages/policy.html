Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here’s how KLFPRO Consulting collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Introduction

At KLFPRO Consulting, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website and services.

We may collect:

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse. Your data is never sold to third parties.
